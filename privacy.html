SimplyAuth

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL. We also store a unique account identifier and device tokens used to deliver push notifications to your device.

If you choose to add a phone number to your profile, we collect and store your verified phone number. Adding a phone number is optional and enables the Call & Verify feature, which lets trusted connections initiate a verification when they call you. Your phone number is verified via a one-time SMS code before it is stored, and is kept private — it is never displayed to other users and is only used server-side to support the calling feature.

When you connect with other users and perform verifications, we store connection records (who connected with whom) and verification records (who verified whom, when, and the outcome). Verification phrases are ephemeral — they expire after 60 seconds and are automatically deleted within 24 hours.

What We Never Collect

Your panic / distress word is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not collect your location, contacts, call logs, or payment card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide real-time identity verification between you and your trusted contacts
  • To send push notifications when a verification request is directed to you
  • To enable the Call & Verify feature — when a connection initiates a call, your phone number is used server-side to support the dialing flow
  • To maintain your list of trusted connections
  • To display your verification history
  • To manage your subscription (Premium tier)

Phone Number Collection and Use

Adding a phone number is entirely optional. If you provide one:

  • It is verified via Firebase Auth SMS before being stored — we never store unverified numbers
  • It is stored privately on our servers and is never visible to other users in the app
  • It is used only to support the Call & Verify feature (allowing connections to initiate a verified call with you)
  • It is not sold, rented, or shared with any third party outside of the Firebase infrastructure used to operate the app
  • It is permanently deleted when you delete your account
  • You can remove it at any time by contacting support@simplyauth.app

Note: Phone numbers can be spoofed by callers. SimplyAuth uses phone numbers as a convenience signal only — always complete the in-app phrase verification before trusting any call.

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase (Alphabet Inc.) — Authentication (including phone number verification via SMS), real-time database, push notifications, and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy

RevenueCat Inc. — Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Apple Inc. / Google LLC — Sign-in and payment processing via their respective platforms.

Data Retention

Verification records are automatically deleted within 24 hours. Your account, connection, subscription, and phone number data is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete Account), which permanently removes all associated data including your phone number. You can also contact us and we will remove your account and all associated records within 30 days.

Security

All data is protected by Firebase Security Rules, which ensure that only you and your verified connections can access shared data. Your phone number is stored server-side only and is never exposed through Firestore security rules or returned to any client other than through authenticated, rate-limited server functions. Verification phrases are never transmitted over the phone — they exist only inside the app and expire automatically.

Children

SimplyAuth is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
